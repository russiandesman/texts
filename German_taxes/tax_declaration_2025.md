# German Tax Declaration 2025 — Step-by-Step Guide

**Situation:** Employee (Angestellter) · German resident · E*TRADE (RSU/ESPP equity compensation) · Capital gains/investments · Filing via ELSTER  
**Deadline:** July 31, 2026 (or February 28, 2027 with a Steuerberater)  
**Tax year:** 2025

---

## Phase 0 — Set Up Your Working Directory

```bash
mkdir tax-2025
cd tax-2025
mkdir banks employer shares
```

Under `banks/`, create one subdir per bank or financial institution you received income from, and drop the annual statements there:

```
tax-2025/
├── banks/
│   ├── deutsche-bank/
│   │   └── jahreskontoauszug_2025.pdf
│   ├── n26/
│   │   └── jahresabschluss_2025.pdf
│   └── ...
├── employer/
│   └── lohnsteuerbescheinigung_2025.pdf
└── shares/
    ├── rsu_2025.xlsx        ← E*TRADE G&L Expanded (RSU)
    ├── espp_2025.xlsx       ← E*TRADE G&L Expanded (ESPP)
    └── eurofier_output_2025.xlsx  ← generated by etrade-eurofier
```

Once all documents are in place, work through the phases below.

---

## Why you must file (Pflichtveranlagung)

You are **required** to file because:
- You have capital gains from a **foreign broker** — unlike German banks (which auto-withhold Abgeltungsteuer), foreign brokers do not deduct German tax. You must self-report all income.
- Any foreign-sourced income triggers a mandatory declaration.

---

## Phase 1 — Gather Documents (do before opening ELSTER)

### From your German employer
- [ ] **Lohnsteuerbescheinigung 2025** — your employer sends this in January/February 2026. Also visible in your ELSTER account under "Bescheinigungen".

### From E*TRADE

Download the **Gain & Loss Expanded** report(s) for 2025 — typically one file per plan (RSU and ESPP are separate):

1. Log in to E*TRADE → **Stock Plan** → **Holdings** or **Tax Center**
2. Select year **2025**, report type **G&L Expanded**, export as **XLSX**
3. Download once for RSUs and once for ESPP if you have both plans

You do **not** need to manually look up exchange rates — the eurofier tool handles that automatically (see Phase 2).

### Other documents
- [ ] Your **Steuernummer** (tax number) — on previous Steuerbescheid or request from Finanzamt
- [ ] Your **IBAN** for any potential refund
- [ ] Any **Freistellungsaufträge** you have placed at German banks — the consumed amount goes into Zeile 16/17 of Anlage KAP (informational only; ELSTER applies the full Sparerpauschbetrag automatically)

## Phase 2 — Run etrade-eurofier

**Tool:** [github.com/russiandesman/etrade-eurofier](https://github.com/russiandesman/etrade-eurofier)

This tool takes your E*TRADE G&L Expanded XLSX files, fetches official ECB exchange rates, applies them per transaction date, and outputs an XLSX with SUMIF totals ready for Anlage KAP.

### Run it

```bash
./convert.py rsu_2025.xlsx espp_2025.xlsx -o eurofier_output_2025.xlsx
```

Pass both files at once if you have RSU and ESPP. Add `--currency USD` if needed (default).

### What to read from the output

Open `eurofier_output_2025.xlsx` and look at the **Result (daily)** sheet — this is the one to use (daily ECB rates, not monthly averages). The SUMIF summary at the top gives you:

| Output field | Maps to ELSTER |
|---|---|
| **Total Gains** (EUR) | Anlage KAP **Zeile 19** (Ausländische Kapitalerträge) — and repeat the same value in **Zeile 20** (sub-line: share disposal gains) |
| **Total Losses** (EUR) | Anlage KAP **Zeile 23** (sub-line: share disposal losses) |
| **Net** (EUR) | Cross-check: Gains minus Losses — ELSTER nets Zeile 20 and Zeile 23 automatically |

> Note: the tool covers equity compensation sales (RSU vests sold, ESPP purchases sold). It handles cost basis correctly — RSUs use vest-date FMV in EUR, ESPP uses full FMV at purchase (not the discounted price) to avoid double-counting the employment benefit already taxed via payroll.

### Also note from the output

- **RSU vest income** (the FMV at vest) is already reported on your Lohnsteuerbescheinigung as part of your salary — do not add it again on Anlage KAP
- **ESPP discount** is similarly already in your payroll — only the gain/loss *after* purchase counts on KAP

### What the tool does NOT cover

- Dividends received (if E*TRADE paid dividends on your shares) — check E*TRADE separately under **Dividends & Interest** in the Tax Center
- US withholding tax on dividends — find this in the same section; you'll need the gross amount and the tax withheld

### Sparerpauschbetrag check

- Allowance: **€1,000** per person (single) / **€2,000** (married filing jointly)
- ELSTER applies it **automatically** — you do not enter it anywhere
- Zeilen 16/17 only report Freistellungsauftrag already consumed at banks (informational)

### Total taxable capital income (your working numbers for ELSTER)

```
E*TRADE gains → Zeile 19 + Zeile 20 (sub-line)
E*TRADE losses → Zeile 23 (sub-line)
Dividends (foreign, no withholding) → Zeile 19
Bank income (no withholding) → Zeile 18
Bank income (with withholding) → Zeile 7
- Sparerpauschbetrag (applied automatically by ELSTER)
= taxable capital income
```

## Phase 3 — Set Up ELSTER

1. Go to **[www.elster.de](https://www.elster.de)** → "Mein ELSTER"
2. Log in with your certificate (`.pfx` file + PIN) or ElsterSecure app
3. If you don't have an account: register at elster.de → takes ~2 weeks to receive activation letter by post — **do this now if not done**
4. Navigate to: **Formulare & Leistungen → Alle Formulare → Einkommensteuererklärung 2025**

---

## Phase 4 — Fill Out the Forms

ELSTER will guide you through a wizard. The key forms for your situation:

### Mantelbogen (main form)
- Personal details, address, marital status
- Enter your **Steuernummer** and **IBAN**
- Tick "Anlage N" and "Anlage KAP" when asked which Anlagen you need

---

### Anlage N — Employment Income

ELSTER can **auto-fill** this from your Lohnsteuerbescheinigung:
1. Click "Daten aus dem Datenabruf übernehmen" to import employer data
2. Verify the pre-filled values match your paper Lohnsteuerbescheinigung
3. Key fields: Zeile 6 (Bruttoarbeitslohn), Zeile 9 (einbehaltene Lohnsteuer), Zeile 10 (Solidaritätszuschlag)

The standard **Arbeitnehmer-Pauschbetrag of €1,230** is applied automatically — no entry or receipt needed. If your actual Werbungskosten exceed €1,230, ELSTER uses the higher amount instead.

**Werbungskosten worth filling in (Section 8, 10, 11, 14):**

| Section | What | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **8 — Entfernungspauschale** | Commuting distance and days per workplace | ELSTER calculates automatically — €0.30/km (first 20 km), €0.38/km beyond; leave Zeilen 51/52 empty if no employer travel subsidy |
| **10 — Arbeitsmittel** | Work equipment not reimbursed by employer | Books, journals, tools — enter each item with amount |
| **11 — Häusliches Arbeitszimmer** | Home office as primary workplace (Mittelpunkt) | €1,260 flat rate (Jahrespauschale) if home is the center of your professional activity |
| **14 — Weitere Werbungskosten** | Miscellaneous work expenses | Kontoführungsgebühr: €16 flat accepted without receipts |

> ⚠️ **Sections 11 and 12 are mutually exclusive.** Section 11 (Arbeitszimmer) and Section 12 (Tagespauschale, €6/day) cover the same concept — ELSTER will flag it if both are filled for the same period. Use section 11 if home is your Mittelpunkt (gives up to €1,260 flat); use section 12 if you split time between home and office (€6 × home days, max €1,260). With fewer than ~30 office days per year, section 11 is justified and gives the same maximum.

**Sections not applicable for a standard employee:**
- Section 13 (double household)
- Section 15 (travel expenses) — unless you have unreimbursed business travel
- Section 17 (special cases) — pensions, multi-year pay, Belgian residency

---

### Anlage KAP — Capital Gains

This is the most important form for your situation. Since your broker is foreign, **none of your capital income had German tax withheld** — you must declare everything here.

**Key lines to fill (2025 form):**

Section 3 — capital income WITH domestic withholding (copy values directly from your bank's Steuerbescheinigung):

| Line | What to enter | Recommended value |
|---|---|---|
| **Zeile 7** | Total bank capital income with withholding | from Steuerbescheinigung |
| **Zeile 8** | Sub-line: share disposal gains contained in Zeile 7 | from Steuerbescheinigung |
| **Zeile 9** | Sub-line: options/futures income contained in Zeile 7 | `0` for standard accounts |
| **Zeile 10** | Sub-line: grandfathered old fund share gains | `0` |
| **Zeile 11** | Sub-line: substitute tax base | `0` |
| **Zeile 12** | Unrecovered non-share losses carried forward by bank | from Steuerbescheinigung |
| **Zeile 13** | Unrecovered share disposal losses carried forward by bank | from Steuerbescheinigung |
| **Zeile 14** | Futures/forwards losses | `0` |
| **Zeile 15** | Write-off / worthless asset losses | `0` |
| **Zeile 16** | Sparerpauschbetrag **already used** via Freistellungsauftrag on section 3 income | `0` if no Freistellungsauftrag set up |
| **Zeile 17** | Sparerpauschbetrag already used elsewhere (KAP-BET/KAP-INV, or combined spouse share if spouse has no Anlage KAP) | `0` if spouse files her own Anlage KAP |

Section 5 — capital income WITHOUT domestic withholding (foreign broker + banks that did not withhold):

| Line | What to enter | Recommended value |
|---|---|---|
| **Zeile 18** | Inländische Kapitalerträge — domestic income with no withholding | from bank statement |
| **Zeile 19** | Ausländische Kapitalerträge — **E*TRADE gross gains here** | from eurofier output |
| **Zeile 20** | Sub-line: share disposal gains contained in Zeile 18/19 | same as Zeile 19 if all gains are from share sales |
| **Zeile 22** | Sub-line: losses excluding share disposal losses | `0` if all losses are from share sales |
| **Zeile 23** | Sub-line: share disposal losses contained in Zeile 18/19 | from eurofier output |
| **Zeile 26** | Finanzamt interest on tax refunds | `0` |
| **Zeile 26a** | Litigation/default interest | `0` |

Section 8 — tax already withheld (credit against your assessment):

| Line | What to enter | Recommended value |
|---|---|---|
| **Zeile 37** | Kapitalertragsteuer withheld by German banks | from Steuerbescheinigung |
| **Zeile 38** | Solidaritätszuschlag withheld (5.5% of Zeile 37 amount) | from Steuerbescheinigung |
| **Zeile 39** | Kirchensteuer on capital gains | `0` unless church member |
| **Zeile 40** | Foreign taxes already credited in a prior assessment | `0` |
| **Zeile 41** | Creditable foreign taxes not yet credited — **US withholding tax on dividends** | from E*TRADE Tax Center |
| **Zeile 42** | Fictitious foreign withholding (treaty deemed credits) | `0` |

> **Sparerpauschbetrag — do NOT enter it anywhere.** ELSTER applies the full €2,000 deduction automatically based on your declared income. You do not claim it, request it, or enter it in any field. Zeilen 16 and 17 are the only related fields, and they are purely informational: enter the amount of Pauschbetrag **already consumed via Freistellungsaufträge** at your banks (read from the Steuerbescheinigung). If you had no Freistellungsauftrag set up at any bank, enter `0` in both — and ELSTER still applies the full €2,000 automatically.

> **Zeile 4 (Günstigerprüfung):** Do **not** tick unless your marginal income tax rate is below 25%. With a regular salary + RSU/ESPP income, your rate is almost certainly above 25%, so ticking it would increase your tax.
>
> **Zeile 5 (Überprüfung des Steuereinbehalts):** **Tick this if you have any income in Zeile 7 — this is mandatory, not optional.** German banks that withheld tax are considered to have already settled it — ELSTER treats re-declaring that income as unusual and requires you to state why. Without ticking Zeile 4 or Zeile 5, ELSTER will block submission entirely with a validation error and refuse to proceed. Zeile 5 is the correct reason here: it instructs ELSTER to review whether the bank's withholding was correct given your full tax picture (Sparerpauschbetrag, losses, etc.), which can result in a refund of over-withheld tax.
> ⚠️ **Pitfall:** Ticking Zeile 5 without filling Zeile 37 (withheld Kapitalertragsteuer) and Zeile 38 (Soli) causes a second validation error. These three always go together — tick Zeile 5 and fill Zeile 37 + 38 from your bank's Steuerbescheinigung at the same time.

**Mocked example values used throughout this document:**

| Item | Amount |
|---|---|
| E*TRADE gross gains (RSU + ESPP sales) | 5,000 EUR |
| E*TRADE losses | 2,000 EUR |
| E*TRADE net | 3,000 EUR |
| Bank income WITH withholding (Zeile 7) | 200 EUR |
| Tax withheld by bank (25% of 200) | 50 EUR |
| Bank income WITHOUT withholding (Zeile 18) | 300 EUR |
| Total bank income | 500 EUR |
| Spouse bank income, no withholding | 157 EUR |
| Sparerpauschbetrag total (married) | 2,000 EUR |
| Freistellungsauftrag already used (Zeile 16/17) | 0 EUR |

**Worked example (mocked values, married filing jointly):**

*Your* Anlage KAP:

| Zeile | Value | Source |
|---|---|---|
| **Zeile 7** | 200 EUR | Bank income WITH withholding |
| **Zeile 16** | 0 EUR | No Freistellungsauftrag used at this bank |
| **Zeile 17** | 0 EUR | Spouse files her own Anlage KAP |
| **Zeile 18** | 300 EUR | Bank income WITHOUT withholding (500 total − 200 in Zeile 7) |
| **Zeile 19** | 5,000 EUR | E*TRADE gross gains (RSU + ESPP) |
| **Zeile 20** | 5,000 EUR | Sub-line: all of Zeile 19 is from share sales |
| **Zeile 23** | 2,000 EUR | Sub-line: share disposal losses |
| **Zeile 37** | 50 EUR | Kapitalertragsteuer withheld by bank (25% of 200 EUR) |


*Spouse's* Anlage KAP:

| Zeile | Value | Source |
|---|---|---|
| **Zeile 5** | leave unticked | No section 3 income — Zeile 5 is only required when Zeile 7 is filled |
| **Zeile 16** | 0 EUR | No Freistellungsauftrag used |
| **Zeile 17** | 0 EUR | You file your own Anlage KAP |
| **Zeile 18** | 157 EUR | Bank income, no withholding (domestic) |

Sparerpauschbetrag flow (both forms combined):
- Zeile 16 (your form): 0 → Zeile 17 (your form): 0 → full **2,000 EUR** flows to section 5 automatically
- Finanzamt splits: 1,843 EUR against your section 5 income, 157 EUR against spouse's section 5 income

Tax outcome on your side:
- Section 3: 200 EUR income, 50 EUR withheld, 0 EUR Freistellungsauftrag used → Finanzamt applies Pauschbetrag from section 5 pool; 50 EUR withheld credited
- Section 5: (300 + 5,000 − 2,000) − 1,843 = 1,457 EUR taxable → ~384 EUR tax
- Net owed: ~384 − 50 = **~334 EUR**

Spouse: 157 − 157 (Pauschbetrag) = **0 EUR taxable**.

> **Anlage KAP-INV:** Required if you hold **investment funds (Investmentfonds/ETFs)**. ELSTER will prompt you. For ETFs, you typically need to declare **Vorabpauschale** — a notional minimum gain even if you didn't sell. Your broker should report this in the annual statement.

**Opting out of flat tax (Günstigerprüfung):**  
If your marginal income tax rate is below 25%, tick "Günstigerprüfung beantragen" (Zeile 4 or nearby). The Finanzamt will automatically apply whichever rate is lower. Usually only relevant if your total income is low.

---

### Anlage AUS — Foreign Income (if needed)

For a **foreign brokerage account**, Anlage AUS is generally **not** required — you declare the income on Anlage KAP. Anlage AUS is for income subject to double taxation treaties where the foreign country has primary taxing rights (e.g., foreign salary, rental income abroad).

However, if your broker is in a country that taxes dividends at source (e.g., US withholding tax of 15% on US stocks), you use **Zeile 41** of Anlage KAP to credit that tax — not Anlage AUS.

## Phase 5 — Review and Submit

1. **Plausibilitätsprüfung:** ELSTER runs automatic checks — fix any red warnings before submitting
2. **Vorschau:** Review the complete PDF before submitting
3. **Übermitteln:** Submit electronically — you'll get a confirmation number (Transferticket)
4. Save the confirmation PDF

---

## Phase 6 — After Submission

- **Steuerbescheid** arrives by post typically 6–12 weeks after submission
- Check it carefully against your declaration
- You have **1 month** to file an **Einspruch** (objection) if something is wrong
- If you receive a refund, it will be transferred to your IBAN

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | What to do instead |
|---|---|
| Double-counting RSU vest income | RSU FMV at vest is already on your Lohnsteuerbescheinigung — only the gain/loss after sale goes on KAP |
| Double-counting ESPP discount | Same — the discount is employment income in payroll; only post-purchase gain goes on KAP |
| Forgetting dividends (not in eurofier output) | Check E*TRADE Tax Center → Dividends & Interest separately |
| Not crediting US withholding tax on dividends | Fill **Zeile 41** of Anlage KAP with taxes already paid to the IRS |
| Assuming E*TRADE has withheld German tax | It has not — you owe Abgeltungsteuer (25% + Soli) on all gains |
| Ticking Zeile 5 without filling Zeile 37/38 | Always fill Zeile 37 (withheld Kapitalertragsteuer) and Zeile 38 (Soli) from the Steuerbescheinigung together with Zeile 5 |
| Missing the deadline | July 31, 2026 — calendar it now |
